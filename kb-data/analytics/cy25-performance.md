# CY25 Performance Results

## Quick Reference

- Revenue: $13,504,185 AUD (+61% YoY), 3.05x growth since CY23
- Bags shipped: 1,007,775 (+63% YoY)
- Paid subscribers: 36,036 (+52% YoY)
- Active subscriptions at year end: 21,685 (+39% YoY)
- SLA performance: 95.5% while scaling 63% more volume

## Revenue

### Revenue Growth

Revenue tripled in three years:

| Year | Revenue (AUD) | Multiple |
|------|--------------|----------|
| CY23 | $4,429,180 | — |
| CY24 | $8,383,318 | 1.89x |
| CY25 | $13,504,185 | 3.05x (vs CY23) |

CY25 revenue increased 61% YoY, driven by [[ftbp|FTBP]] which now accounts for 41% of the revenue mix.

### Revenue Mix Transformation

| Revenue Stream | CY24 | CY25 |
|----------------|------|------|
| FTBP Paid | 3% | **41%** |
| Beanz Subscription | 36% | 33% |
| Fusion | 45% | 19% |
| Nonsubscription | 6% | 5% |
| Coffee Essentials | 9% | 2% |
| PBB | 0% | 0% |
| **Total** | **$8.3M AUD** | **$13.5M AUD** |

The revenue mix shifted dramatically: FTBP Paid went from 3% to 41%, while Fusion declined from 45% to 19%. This reflects the strategic shift toward machine-attached coffee subscriptions.
