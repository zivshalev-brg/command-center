# Cancellation KPIs

Tracks revenue and volume attributed to cancelled subscribers using PBI Cancellation Measures sourced from FactCancelationSurvey. Measures the economic impact of churned subscribers by capturing their completed orders, revenue, and unit volume before and after cancellation.

## Data Notes

- **Cancellation Survey** data comes from `FactCancelationSurvey`, which links cancelled subscriptions to their order history.
- **Revenue (Cancellation Survey)** measures total revenue generated by customers who subsequently cancelled. This is historical revenue, not lost revenue.
- **Units in KG** provides weight-based volume for cancelled subscriber orders, enabling comparison with overall unit metrics in [[kpi-revenue]].
- For churn rate and subscription cancellation counts, see [[kpi-subscriptions]]. For market-level churn reasons, see the `kpi-churn-{market}` docs.

## PBI Measures

This doc draws from the **Cancellation Measures** folder in BeanzCoreDatabricks:

- Actual Revenue (Cancellation Survey)
- Sum of Completed Orders (Cancellation Survey)
- Sum of Completed Units (Cancellation Survey)
- Sum of Completed Units in KG (Cancellation Survey)
