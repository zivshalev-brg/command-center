# Customer KPIs

Tracks the customer base across all beanz.com markets using PBI Customer Measures. Covers total customer counts, paid/free splits, subscription vs on-demand behavior, FTBP customer breakdown, new customer acquisition with YoY comparison, LTV, and recency distribution.

## Data Notes

- **All Customers** counts unique `DimCustomerEmail` records. This includes customers who only received free bags and never paid.
- **Free Bags Only** (`kpi_customers_free_bags_only`) identifies customers with zero paid orders — useful for measuring FTBP-to-paid conversion opportunity.
- **FTBP %** fields measure FTBP customer penetration by order type (subscription, on-demand, both). These use `DimCustomerEmail[CustomerFTBPStatus]`.
- **Recency buckets** are mutually exclusive time windows based on last paid order date. They sum to `kpi_customers_completed_orders`.
- **Customer LTV** is calculated at the customer level (across all subscriptions). For subscription-level LTV, see [[kpi-subscriptions]].

## PBI Measures

This doc draws from the **Customer Measures** folder in BeanzCoreDatabricks:

- Count of All Customers
- Count of Customers - Completed Orders (+ LY, % Change)
- Count of Customers - Paid Bags / Free Bags / Free Bags Only
- Count of Customers with Subscription / Non Subscription / Both Orders
- Count of FTBP Customers (total, Sub Only, On Demand Only, Both)
- % of FTBP Customers (total, Subscription, On Demand, Both)
- Count of New Customers (+ LY, % Change, Converted to Paid)
- Customer Lifetime Value
- Average Orders / Units per Customer
- Recency: 30d, 31-90d, 91-182d, 183-365d, >365d
