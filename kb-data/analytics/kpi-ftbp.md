# FTBP KPIs

Tracks the Fast-Track Barista Pack full funnel using the DS_FTBP PBI model. Covers eligible registrations through signups, opt-ins/opt-outs, conversions (free bag and subscription paths), and milestone payouts. All metrics available with v1/v2 program version splits.

## Data Notes

- **DS_FTBP** is a separate PBI model from BeanzCoreDatabricks, with its own refresh schedule. Check `kpi_pbi_refresh` for currency.
- **v1 vs v2**: FTBP v1 offered a single free bag; v2 offers two free bags plus subscription incentives. All funnel metrics are split by version.
- **Signup Rate** = Signups / Submitted Sign-Ups. **Opt-in Rate** = Eligible Registration Opt-in Rate (registrations that proceed to signup).
- **Conversion paths**: Free Bags path (receive free bags, then convert to paid order) vs Subscription path (sign up directly for subscription). Each has distinct conversion rates.
- **FBO (Free 2 Bags Only)**: Customers who received free bags but have not yet made a paid order. FBO conversion rate tracks their eventual paid order conversion.
- **6-Week Conversion Rate** measures paid order conversion within the first 6 weeks of FTBP registration, providing an early-signal metric.
- **Milestone Payout** tracks customers who reached the first reward milestone (roaster partner commission trigger).

## PBI Measures

This doc draws from the **FTBPRegistrationMeasures** folder in DS_FTBP:

- Eligible Product Registrations (total, v1, v2)
- Submissions, Submitted Sign-Ups (total, v1, v2)
- Sign-Ups (total, Free Bags, Subscription, v1/v2 splits)
- Sign-Up Rate (total, v1, v2), Opt-in Rate
- % Free Bag / Subscription Sign-Ups
- Conversions (total, Free, Sub, v1/v2 splits), Conversion Rates
- FBO Conversions / Rates, Subscription to Paid Conversions / Rates
- Registrations With Paid Order (total, 6-week window), Conversion Rates
- Subscription / FBO Registration counts and rates
- Eligible Registrations, Total Registrations
- Opt-Outs / Rates (total, v1, v2)
- Rejected Sign-Ups (total, v1, v2), Rejections, % Rejected
- Milestone Payout count and %
