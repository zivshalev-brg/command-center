# Shipment KPIs

Tracks fulfillment volume using PBI Shipment Measures. Covers shipment order counts with last-month and last-year comparisons, non-cancelled shipments, purchase-order-level aggregation, shipment percentage metrics, unit volume, and total shipping fees.

## Data Notes

- **Shipment Orders** counts distinct shipment records from FactShipment, not the same as completed orders in [[kpi-revenue]] (one order can have multiple shipments).
- **Not Canceled** excludes shipments with a cancelled status, providing the effective fulfillment count.
- **By PO** aggregates at the purchase order level rather than individual shipment level.
- **Shipment Order %** (LM, LY variants) measures shipment volume as a percentage of total orders, useful for tracking fulfillment efficiency over time.
- **Total Shipping Fee** is the fee charged to the customer, distinct from shipping cost to Breville in [[kpi-revenue]].

## PBI Measures

This doc draws from the **Shipment Measures** folder in BeanzCoreDatabricks:

- Count of Shipment Orders (+ LM, LY, Not Canceled, By PO)
- Shipment Order % (+ LM, LY)
- Sum of Units - Shipments
- Total Shipping Fee
