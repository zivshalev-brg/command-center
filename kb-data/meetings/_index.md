# Meetings

Meeting intelligence reports generated by the meeting-analyzer skill. Each report captures action items, decisions, argument maps, sentiment analysis, and social dynamics from team meetings.

## Domain Scope

**What belongs here:** Meeting reports with structured intelligence extraction (action items, decisions, arguments, sentiment, dynamics).

**Project-scoped meetings** live in `docs/projects/{project}/meetings/`. This folder contains only cross-cutting meetings not tied to a specific project.

**What does NOT belong here:** Ad-hoc notes, agenda documents, or raw transcripts. Those go in `_research/`.
